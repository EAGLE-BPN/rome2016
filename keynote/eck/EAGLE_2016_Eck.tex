Werner Eck is an internationally renowned historian of the Roman Empire and of Roman epigraphy. From February 1979 until his retirement in February 2007, he worked as Professor of Ancient History at the University of Cologne. From 1997 till 2002 he was president of the Association of Greek and Latin Epigraphy. At the Accademy of Sciences in Berlin he is responsible for the CIL and the PIR. With his colleagues of the Berlin-Brandenburgische Akademie der Wissenschaften he organised the 13th International Congress of Greek and Latin Epigraphy.
